DRL Baseline Reimplementation

This is my final project for CSE 573 (Artificial Intelligence). I reimplemented 5 deep reinforcement learning algorithms (A2C, DDPG, PPO, TD3, SAC) in Pytorch and carried out a serial of experiments on OpenAi gym environment (HalfCheetah-v2 and Hopper-v2) to investigate the effects of different aspects on the performance of DRL models.

Vehicles Pose Estimation based on CenterNet

This is my final project for CSE 599G1 (Deep Learning) and it is also a Kaggle competition. In this project, we applied CentetNet to predict vehicles' 6D (x, y, z, roll, pitch, yaw) pose given a single 2D image. Our main contribution is combining different features and criterion for different components of the pose to improve the prediction result.

DogGAN

This is my project for CSE 455 (Computer Vision). We first built a small dataset of Shiba Inu with around 300 images. Then we tried out different GANs to make use of such a small dataset to get reasonable results. Basically, we tried 3 ideas: providing extract information like edges with Pix2Pix, transferring human features to dogs with AttGAN and training on single image with SinGAN.
